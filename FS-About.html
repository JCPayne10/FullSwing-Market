About FullSwing

Who We Are

FullSwing Farm is a four-season market garden located in Fanklin County NC and servicing the local community. Local restaurants and families alike are welcome as long as we have the produce.

What I am doing

FullSwing Farm was started in the summer of 2020 by Franklinton native Ian Draughn in his native Franklin County. After seeing a need to provide fresh pesticide free produce to his family, Draughn took the matters into his own hands and used skills learned at his apprenticeship with Kyle lastName of Humble Roots Farm in Wilmington, NC.

Why it's Important

The importance of growing sustainably and close to the source is so understated. People gain so much by being close to their food source. It makes you part of the ecosystem.
